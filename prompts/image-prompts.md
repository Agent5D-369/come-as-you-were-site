# COME AS YOU WERE — visual development prompts

## 1) Hero / OG image
Dark cinematic horizon under deep indigo night sky. A lone silhouette stands above a city of flashbulbs, backstage doors, and distant emergency lights. Mood is elegiac, dangerous, and expensive. No celebrity likenesses. No text. Photorealistic with subtle film grain and premium thriller energy. Wide composition with strong negative space. 1200x630.

## 2) Limited series poster
Premium prestige-thriller poster. One central silhouette in a long coat facing a foggy urban skyline. Faint stage lights, police tape geometry, cassette static, and overexposed paparazzi flashes merging into the sky. Palette: black, indigo, gold, steel blue, ember red. No text. No real celebrity likenesses. Cinematic, awards-season, emotionally charged. 1600x900.

## 3) Audio companion cover
Minimalist investigative podcast cover. Clean dark background with torn-paper layers, analog waveform distortion, a single microphone glow, and subtle stage-light haze. Suggest corruption of narrative, not sensational gore. No text. Square cover. Premium editorial design aesthetic.

## 4) Investor deck divider art
Abstract high-end texture using analog static, faint guitar-amp light bleed, forensic document overlays, and gold/blue halo gradients. No text. Designed as a subtle divider background for a dark investor deck.
