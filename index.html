What the series is

A 6-part prestige limited series with a clean commercial engine

The story begins with the death of a world-famous musician. The ruling is swift. The grief is public. The mythology hardens fast. But behind the headlines is a circle of lovers, handlers, fixers, investigators, executives, friends, and opportunists — each holding a different version of the truth, and each with a reason to defend it.

The central dramatic question evolves from Who killed him? to something far stickier and more fundable: Who needed the story to close, and what did it cost to keep it closed?
