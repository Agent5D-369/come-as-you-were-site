Investor notes

Come As You Were

This project works best when it is pitched as a premium fictionalized mystery with cross-format upside, not as a reckless direct-accusation film.

What makes it attractive

  • Recognizable cultural ghost without needing a literal-name dependency.
  • Strong spoken-title test for series, podcast, and talent conversations.
  • Actor-bait roles across investigator, partner, fixer, label, friend, and media ecosystem lanes.
  • Flexible entry points for producer attachment, optioning, EP finance, or podcast partnership.

What not to do

  • Do not market it like a fan-made conspiracy shrine.
  • Do not present unverified allegations as fact.
  • Do not over-explain the reference in the title. Let the wink do its work.
  • Do not bury the commercial angle under “visionary” fog.

Creator involvement lanes

Rick Broider can be positioned as creator, package originator, producer-track attachment, consulting producer, option participant, executive producer, or rights partner depending on how the financing and development path is structured.
