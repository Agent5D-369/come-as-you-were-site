Private package brief

COME AS YOU WERE

A premium 6-part limited series inspired by Kurt Cobain, Nirvana, Seattle 1994, and the war over the official story after a generation-defining musician dies.

Logline

When the death of a world-defining rock star is ruled shut, a private investigator collides with the widow, the inner circle, the label, and the city that built the myth — and discovers the real crime may be the story that survived.

Format

6 x 50 minute limited series. Prestige thriller / music mythology / contaminated-truth drama.

Story in one page

The series uses the real cultural pressure points around Kurt Cobain and Nirvana as ignition: the Rome overdose, the rehab intervention, the disappearance, the locked room, the note, the widow, the private investigator, the money, and the decades of argument that followed. But it is structured as a fictionalized prestige drama rather than a literal courtroom brief, which gives it wider castability, stronger legal footing, and more room to become an emotionally devastating series rather than a tabloid reenactment.

Episode one begins before the death, when the anti-fame icon is already collapsing under addiction, body pain, marriage strain, media pressure, and the machine built around Nirvana. The season then intercuts three timelines: the last days, the missing days, and the aftermath. What starts as a death mystery becomes a study in narrative control: who benefited from closure, who profited from myth, who shaped the widow story, who shaped the martyr story, and what happened to Kurt the person once Kurt the symbol became more useful.

The engine is not “solve the murder.” The engine is that every person in the circle may be telling the truth from inside their own incentive structure. That is what gives the widow role, the investigator role, and the whole inner-circle ecosystem real dramatic voltage.
