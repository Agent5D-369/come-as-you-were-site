Investor / producer FAQ

COME AS YOU WERE

Is this literally about Kurt Cobain?

It is directly inspired by the Kurt Cobain / Nirvana mythology and uses those pressure points as the commercial ignition, but it is designed to function as a fictionalized prestige series rather than a literal allegation project.

Why not make it a documentary?

Because the premium-series opportunity is in dramatizing the widow, the investigator, the inner circle, the label, the estate, and the economics of closure. Drama creates bigger roles, bigger casting opportunities, and broader audience reach.

What makes this more than fandom?

The underlying case has retained public fascination for decades because it fuses music history, addiction, marriage warfare, media manipulation, money, and distrust of official narratives. That is bigger than nostalgia.

What is the real thematic hook?

A death becomes a product. The series is about what happens when public grief, private incentives, and cultural myth all start feeding each other.

What are the best comps?

Use tonal coordinates rather than lazy direct comparisons: a contaminated-truth thriller with the obsession pressure of a prestige investigation, the emotional corrosion of intimate character drama, and the iconography of a major music-world story.

How can I participate?

Potential paths include development financing, producer attachment, banner packaging, rights/option structure, talent access, and companion audio expansion.
