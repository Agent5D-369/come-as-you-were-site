Investor & producer FAQ

The practical questions

Is this meant for Netflix specifically?

No. The project is designed to be buyer-flexible. The strongest path is through producers, reps, attorneys, managers, and packaging partners with existing buyer relationships across streamers and premium outlets.

What is the core asset?

The core asset is the packageable concept and its lane: a fictionalized prestige limited series with a culturally loaded hook, castable roles, built-in press energy, and companion-media upside.

What makes this different from fan-theory clutter?

Discipline. The project is not selling certainty about a real-world allegation. It is selling a premium dramatic investigation into how truth gets distorted around fame, grief, money, and control.

What materials are ready now?

Public pitch site, package brief, call-sheet language, investor FAQ, visual prompts, and a launch-ready structure for private deck, treatment, and attachment outreach.

Where does the money case live?

In the packaging. Recognizable category. Press-friendly premise. Awards-level roles. Companion audio and media expansion. Rights-aware fictionalization that broadens the buyer pool instead of shrinking it.

What happens next if I am interested?

Request the private deck, book a call, and identify your lane: packaging, development finance, talent access, rights strategy, or companion-media expansion.
