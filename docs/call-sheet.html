Phone pitch weapon

COME AS YOU WERE

Open with this

It is a premium six-part limited series inspired by Kurt Cobain, Nirvana, Seattle 1994, the widow, the investigator, and the official story that closed too fast. Think music mythology plus contaminated-truth thriller, not documentary recap.

Then say this

The hook is not just “what happened to Kurt.” The hook is who needed the story to close, who benefited from closure, and what it cost to turn a dying man into a clean myth.

Fast bullets

  • 6 x 50 minutes
  • Kurt / Nirvana cultural heat without literal tabloid framing
  • Awards-grade widow role and obsessive PI role
  • Rome overdose, rehab escape, missing days, note, estate, handlers, label pressure, narrative war
  • Built for producer packaging and streamer rooms, not direct unsolicited fantasy
  • Series + companion podcast extension

Questions to ask the other side

  • Do you see this more as streamer package, premium cable package, or prestige indie setup?
  • Would you want to lead with showrunner attachment, actress attachment, or banner attachment?
  • Does the fictionalized lane increase your confidence versus a direct-name rights fight?
  • Who is the first buyer or talent conversation you hear in your head?
